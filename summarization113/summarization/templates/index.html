About AI Video Summarizer

The AI Video Summarizer helps you quickly extract the most meaningful insights from videos. It offers two key features:

  • Static Summary: Get summaries from predefined videos instantly.
  • Dynamic Upload: Upload any video and receive a real-time summary powered by AI.

This tool is ideal for students, professionals, and busy individuals looking to review video content efficiently. It uses advanced machine learning models to identify key points, saving you time and effort.
